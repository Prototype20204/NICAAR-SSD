About Us

Nile Institute for Climate Adaptation and Agricultural Research (NICAAR) is a South Sudan-based private agricultural, rural and development, environmental, and natural resources research institute. Founded in December 2023.Anchored on its core values and mission statement, NICAAR’s vision is to be a leading private agricultural and rural development, knowledge management, and climate change adaptation research center in South Sudan and in the Nile Basin region, with an ultimate goal of “economic growth and enhance sustainable livelihoods and agri-food systems for both urban and rural populations not only in South Sudan but also in the Nile Basin countries through research in climate change adaptation, agriculture and environment”.

Mission

To be a leading private agricultural and rural development, knowledge management, and climate change adaptation research center in South Sudan and in the Nile Basin Region.

Vision

To spur economic growth and enhance sustainable livelihoods and agri-food systems for both urban and rural populations not only in South Sudan but also in the Nile Basin countries through research in climate change adaptation, agriculture and environment.

Objectives

To implement the National Agenda for Resuscitation of Sustainable Environment, Agri-food systems, Food Security and Livelihoods (NARSEASFL)

Values

• Quality research (Data-driven Knowledge Creation, Management & Sharing)
